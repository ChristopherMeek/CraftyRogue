Registered Education Provider (REP)

The Scrum Alliance developed a program for instructors and training organizations that offer certification and non-certification courses pertinent to agile and Scrum professional. The Scrum Alliance Registered Education Provider (REP) program provides organizations affiliation with and endorsement by the Scrum Alliance. It also gives these organizations access to course advertisement services on the Scrum Alliance website.

The REP Program and You

For the Scrum and agile community, the Scrum Alliance REP program yields access to a variety of course offerings designed to foster the skills needed to succeed and improve at Scrum and other agile-related methodologies.

  • Scrum Alliance Registered Education Provider (REP) organizations are evaluated to ensure that they employ well-qualified instructors.
  • All Scrum Alliance REP course content is screened to verify that the material is consistent with Scrum and agile principles.
  • Certain Scrum Alliance REP courses are designated as CSD-track courses. As such, they can be applied toward the new Certified Scrum Developer (CSD) credential.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the fees?

Fees range from $1,000-$2,000 per year. See the Scrum Alliance REP application package for full details. There is also a $250 course review fee.

What are the qualifications?

Scrum Alliance REP instructors must demonstrate an ability to convey their expertise through courses pertinent to agile practitioners. These courses must be approved prior to publication on our site. Complete details are available in the application package.

Are CSD and REP connected?

The Certified Scrum Developer program is a curriculum of courses that offer the skills developers and other technical-minded team members need to succeed. All courses in the CSD curriculum are taught by Scrum Alliance Registered Education Providers.

Have more questions? Find the answers you need here or feel free to contact us directly.

Become a REP

To apply:

  1. Download and complete the application. This packet includes complete details on the fees, learning objectives, and required documentation as well as instructions for submission.
  2. Create a .ZIP archive/package containing your application and any supporting documents.
  3. Upload your application to YouSendIt.com or mail a hard copy to the address listed in the application packet.
  4. Pay your $250 Course Review Fee.
  5. Notify our support team about your submission by sending an email to REPsupport@scrumalliance.org.

Contact us with questions concerning the REP Program application process.
